Why RxConnect

Designed to make daily healthcare work more productive

RxConnect is not only a prescription form. It is a connected workspace where doctors, clinics, and pharmacies can move faster with less manual follow-up and better operational clarity.

For doctors

Doctors can search and select the patient, review the patient’s history and PMR, prescribe medicines, and issue the prescription from the same environment. This reduces switching between pages and helps clinicians work with more confidence and speed.

  • View patient details and prescribing context in one place
  • Write prescriptions with less back-and-forth and better continuity
  • Benefit when prescriptions are dispensed through Chobham Pharmacy

For clinics

Clinics gain a cleaner onboarding and administration model. Each clinic can join with the correct licensing information, create an admin user, and manage doctors working under that clinic.

  • Structured clinic setup and approval flow
  • Doctor management under one clinic context
  • Improved operational control for prescription activity

For pharmacies

Pharmacies can dispense prescriptions through a clear digital flow using the prescription code and patient date of birth. This makes dispensing easier, reduces confusion, and supports a faster handoff from doctor to patient to pharmacy.

  • Simple code-based lookup for dispensing
  • Less friction during prescription collection
  • Clearer connection with the prescribing workflow

Platform workflow

How the experience works in practice

RxConnect supports the full prescription path, from the first patient selection to the final dispensing step.

01

Select the patient

The doctor chooses the patient from a managed list or creates a new patient profile when needed.

02

Review history and PMR

Patient details, previous prescription records, and history data can be reviewed before prescribing.

03

Create the e-prescription

The doctor adds medicines, dosage instructions, notes, and issues the prescription through the same workflow.

04

Dispense smoothly

The pharmacy uses the code-based flow for easier dispensing, while the platform keeps the process visible.
