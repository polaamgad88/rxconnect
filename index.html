About Us

built for doctors, pharmacists & patients

We streamline the prescription lifecycle: from a doctor filling an accurate e-prescription, to a centralized list for audit and search, to a detailed view for pharmacists and patients. Role-based access, audit trails, and validation rules reduce errors and improve safety.
