How Does Our Electronic Prescribing System Work?

As the world moves more towards digital services, your patients expect efficiency and convenience to help them work around their busy lives.

We’ve developed a system for electronic prescribing that works much the same as the NHS EPS, targeted to make your life easier – whether you’re consulting in person, or remotely.

RxConnect is easy to use and complies with the Medicines for Human Use (Prescribing) Order 2005, allowing prescriptions to be signed using advanced electronic signatures.

Register

Grab your smartphone and your passport or driving licence and register to our prescribing service through a simple, one-off registration.

Prescribe

Log in securely using two-factor authentication and create a prescription by adding a new patient or searching for an existing patient.

Issue

Your patient receives a code by email so they can collect their prescription from any UK pharmacy or arrange an online prescription delivery service.

Track

To view your patient’s prescription history or see the status of the prescription and where it has been collected, just check your private account.

What Is the Cost?

Our digital prescribing platform uses a simple credit system that allows you to purchase prescription credits individually or in bulk — whichever suits your workflow best.

Each prescription credit costs £1 + VAT, making it more affordable than posting a physical prescription and far more efficient for busy practices.
