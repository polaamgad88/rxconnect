Implementation notes

What this billing page now supports

  • Track Chobham reimbursements separately from other-pharmacy charges.
  • Show the prescriber’s net monthly position clearly for review.
  • Keep the external-pharmacy flow visible for operational follow-up.

Current billing logic

Every prescription dispensed with Chobham Pharmacy adds £1.00 to the prescriber. Every prescription dispensed by another pharmacy creates a £0.55 charge that is deducted from the prescriber’s net total.
